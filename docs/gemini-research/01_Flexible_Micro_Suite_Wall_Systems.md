## User prompt
I am repurposing a retail space of 1200 sf into 5 100sf plus 3 150sf micro suites with the rest as a lobby, what wall systems are available to make these units flexible

## Wall system options (summary)
1. **Modular demountable** (DIRTT, ALUR, Muraflex, KI) — high flexibility, STC comparable to drywall; ~$45–$110/sf wall face; ALUR glass fronts ~$400/LF installed entry tier
2. **Operable partitions** (Modernfold, Hufcor, Skyfold) — instant open/close; ~$15–$110+/sf; need overhead structure; weaker lockable security
3. **Industrial modular panels** (Panel Built, Porta-King) — budget; ~$10–$35/sf
4. **Sliding / barn / pocket fronts** — saves 9–14 sf per swing door (~10% of a 100 sf suite)

## Build notes for bank / CapEx
- Prefer demountable over drywall for reconfiguration without demolition
- Target STC ≥ 40 for suite privacy
- Integrate power/data chase in panels
- Per-suite HVAC supply/return (or shared plenum) + sprinkler/alarm coverage per enclosed suite (NFPA)
- Cost-efficiency tip: solid Panel Built between suites + ALUR glass corridor fronts

## Cost efficiency tip (from chat)
For 1,200 sf: solid panels between suites + glass facade on corridor.
