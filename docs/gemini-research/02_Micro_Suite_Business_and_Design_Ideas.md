## Scope
Tenant use-cases for **100–200 sf** micro-suites + shared common space; utilities installed for renters.

## Primary tenant categories (Beauty & Personal Care)
Beautician, Hairdresser, Barber, Tattoo/Permanent Makeup, Makeup Artist, Lash Tech, Nail Tech, Esthetician, Eyebrow Specialist, Spray Tan — with typical FF&E needs (sink, ventilation, mirrors, treatment beds, sterilizable surfaces).

Also viable: creative/media, education, wellness/fitness, specialty retail/crafting, light tech services.

## Additional fit businesses (from chat)
Acupuncturist, hypnotherapist, aromatherapist, seamstress/tailor, apparel heat-press, florist, notary, life coach, financial consultant.

## Design concepts (3 suites + common)
1. **Beauty & Wellness Hub** — stylist (sink/mirror), specialist (private table), creator (vanity/vent)
2. **Creative & Tech Zone** — podcast (soundproof), graphic/video lab, photo nook
3. **Hobby & Instruction** — art corner, retail boutique, maker workshop

## Shared common / utilities (landlord provides)
- Waiting / lobby seating; optional front desk
- Restroom(s)
- Utility room (mop sink, washer/dryer for linens, cleaning storage)
- Kitchenette (microwave, mini-fridge, coffee)
- Per-suite: dedicated breaker preferred; metered electric ideal; high-speed Wi-Fi to all suites
